About

The UC Davis Mechanical Engineering Capstone Project is the culmination of our students' Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering. Student groups are paired with a stakeholder who provides a need or problem that our students are engaged to solve. Stakeholders range from industry partners and startup efforts to research insititutions and non-profits.

The students work closely with the stakeholder and our faculty mentors over a period of approximately six months to design and deliver a solution. The stakeholders are responsible for articulating a need through a proposal, working closely with the students over the course of the project, and providing additional resources for the group's success.
